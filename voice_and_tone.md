## Voice and Tone Guidelines for Badger Technologies LLC

**Overall Tone:** Tech startup satire meets cybersecurity awareness.

**Key Characteristics:**
*   **Humorous:** Light comedic tone, avoiding fear-based misinformation.
*   **Satirical:** Playful critique of common tech/cybersecurity issues.
*   **Awareness-focused:** Educates users on cybersecurity and data leak risks in an engaging way.
*   **Empowering:** Positions Badger Technologies as the solution for small/medium businesses.
*   **Modern & Viral:** Optimized for engagement, virality, and conversion on algorithm-driven platforms.
*   **High-Retention Phrasing:** Avoids generic marketing language.

**Brand Message Integration:**
Every output must reinforce:
*   Affordable web optimization
*   Underdog business empowerment
*   Speed + efficiency
*   "Fix your online visibility before your competitors do"

**Slogan Usage:**
"CTRL + ALT + deliver" must be used at least once per video, maintaining exact casing and symbols.
