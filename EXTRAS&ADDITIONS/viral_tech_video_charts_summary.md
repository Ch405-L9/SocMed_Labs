## Chart 1 – Distribution of Viral Tech Videos by Platform (Sample Set)

This chart shows how many viral short-form tech videos (in a small sample) came from each platform.

- TikTok: 7 videos
- Instagram Reels: 6 videos

Key implications:
- TikTok leads the pack for viral tech content volume.
- Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts are close behind and should mirror TikTok patterns with minor platform tuning.

## Chart 2 – Content Format Mix in Viral Tech Videos (Sample Set)

This pie chart shows the mix of content formats used across the viral tech videos in the sample. Each slice is roughly 16–20% of the total, meaning no single format dominates.

Formats included:
- AI tool demo (faceless)
- Educational 5‑tip breakdown
- POV story: client result
- Problem–solution reveal
- Before/after funnel teardown
- Face cam + b‑roll code walkthrough
- Faceless screen share tutorial

Key implications:
- Viral tech content succeeds across multiple formats; you are not locked into a single style.
- Rotating formats (demos, POV stories, teardowns, faceless tutorials) keeps the feed fresh while staying on brand.
- The system should treat format choice as a lever: for each idea, it can pick one of these formats based on platform and goal (awareness, authority, lead generation).
