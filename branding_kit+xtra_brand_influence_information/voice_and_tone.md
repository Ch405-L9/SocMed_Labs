## Brand Personality
BADGR is the neighborhood tech expert — the person Atlanta SMBs wish they had on staff.
Not corporate. Not a bloated agency. Sharp, direct, results-first.

**Tone pillars:**
- **Confident** — We know our stuff. No hedging, no "might", no "could possibly".
- **Direct** — No fluff. Lead with the point.
- **Local** — Atlanta is home. I-285, I-85, Lawrenceville, Gwinnett — these are real references, not generic "city" placeholders.
- **Accessible** — No jargon overload. SMB owners don't know what "microservices architecture" means and don't need to.
- **Results-first** — Lead with outcomes, not features. "We tripled their leads" before "we rebuilt their site".

## Voice Examples

### DO write like this:
- "Your Atlanta website is on page 4. We fix that."
- "AI handled 80% of this client's customer questions overnight. No staff. No phone."
- "We built BADGR Bolt — our own Android app, live on the Play Store. We ship things."
- "DM us CTRL. Free 15-minute audit. Atlanta businesses only."

### DON'T write like this:
- "Welcome to our channel! Today we're going to be talking about..."
- "As a leading provider of digital transformation solutions..."
- "Our synergistic approach to web optimization leverages cutting-edge methodologies..."
- "Hey guys, so I wanted to share something really cool today!"
