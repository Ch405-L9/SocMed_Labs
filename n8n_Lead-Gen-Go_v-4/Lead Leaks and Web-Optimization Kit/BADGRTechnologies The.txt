II. Operational Mechanics: How It Works
The agency leverages local AI orchestration (Gemini CLI) to run up to six parallel tasks, such as simultaneous market research, content generation, and legal drafting
.
Lead Acquisition Engine: The agency uses a "Lead Leak Scan" protocol to identify technical failures in prospect sites (e.g., slow mobile speed, HTTPS issues, or ADA non-compliance) and offers three specific 14-day fixes to build trust
.
FOSS-First Tech Stack: The operation is designed for zero-cost scalability using Free and Open Source Software (FOSS), including Next.js for landing pages, HubSpot for CRM, and a custom SERP Micro API for lead discovery without paid API costs
.
Conversion Weaponization: Pain points are converted into a CSV-driven outreach system that maps specific site failures (like 3-second load times) to ROI-driven messaging
.
