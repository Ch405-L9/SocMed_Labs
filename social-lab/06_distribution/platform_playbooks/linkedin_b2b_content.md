# linkedin_b2b_content

## Why LinkedIn for BADGR

LinkedIn reaches the decision-makers TikTok and Reels won't — managing partners, practice administrators, and office managers who control vendor decisions. A viral TikTok gets views; a LinkedIn post gets a DM from someone who has budget and a problem.

This isn't a volume platform. One strong post per week that reaches 5 practice managers is worth more than 20 Reels views from people who'll never hire you.

## Algorithm Rules (2026)

- **Ranking signals:** Comments → Shares → Reactions → Dwell time (time spent reading)
- Comments from 1st-degree connections in first 60 minutes = massive distribution boost
- Native video (uploaded directly) gets 3–5x more reach than a YouTube/TikTok link
- Text-only posts with a strong hook outperform image posts — algorithm rewards dwell time
- External links (in the post body) suppress reach; put links in first comment instead
- Posting frequency sweet spot: 2–3x/week; daily posting on LinkedIn often backfires

## Content Format Guide

| Format | Best For | Engagement Type |
|--------|---------|----------------|
| Native short video (30–90s) | Before/after demos, myth-busts | Comments + shares |
| Text-only post with hook | Opinions, case observations, myth-busts | Comments (highest) |
| Document/PDF carousel | Checklists, compliance guides, pricing breakdowns | Saves + shares |
| Image with caption | Quick stats, before/after screenshots | Reactions |
| Article (LinkedIn native) | Deep dives, thought leadership | Profile views + follows |

**Best format for BADGR:** Text-only hook post → native video → document carousel (in that priority order)

## Post Structure That Works

```
[Hook line — bold claim, question, or counter-intuitive stat]

[2–3 sentences expanding the hook — the setup]

[Bullet list or numbered steps — the value]

[1-line takeaway]

[CTA — soft ask, not "book now"]

#hashtag1 #hashtag2 #hashtag3
```

**Example:**
```
Most Atlanta law firm websites are leaking intake calls and the owners don't know it.

I've scanned 12 sites this quarter. Every single one had at least one of these:

• Broken contact form (mobile only — never tested)
• HTTPS missing on the intake page
• 6+ second mobile load time
• No call button visible without scrolling

None of these are expensive to fix. All of them are costing real cases.

If you run a small firm and haven't had your site scanned in 12 months, it's worth 10 minutes to check.

Link to a free triage checklist in the comments.

#AtlantaBusiness #LawFirmMarketing #WebsiteCompliance
```

## Hashtag Bank

**Always include:**
`#AtlantaBusiness` or `#Atlanta`

**Rotate 2–3:**
`#LawFirmMarketing` `#MedicalPractice` `#WebsiteCompliance` `#TechnicalSEO` `#DigitalMarketing` `#SmallBusinessTips` `#HIPAA` `#ADA`

## Posting Schedule

- Tue, Thu (primary) — 9:00–11:00 AM EST (peak decision-maker browsing)
- Sat (optional) — use for softer content (behind-the-scenes, ops clips)
- Never post and disappear — reply to all comments within 2 hours of posting

## The LinkedIn Long Game

LinkedIn content compounds differently than TikTok — posts resurface in searches and feed for weeks. A single strong post about HIPAA web compliance could generate inbound DMs for 30 days. Priority is quality over cadence here.

Target: 1 strong post per week + 1 native video per week = enough to stay visible with the right audience.
