# instagram_reels_2026_playbook

## Algorithm Rules (2026)

- **Ranking signals (in order):** Saves → Shares → Watch-through rate → Comments → Likes
- Saves are weighted 3–5x more than likes — every CTA should push saves
- Engagement from non-followers drives Explore distribution; this is where growth happens
- Cross-sharing a Reel to Stories within 24 hours adds +15–30% reach to the original
- Collab posts (tagging a partner account) doubles the audience pool with no paid spend
- Instagram now favors accounts that post both Reels AND carousels — mix formats weekly

## Growth Levers

1. **Carousel posts** — compliance checklists, before/after metrics tables — get 2x saves vs. Reels for text-heavy content
2. **Collab posts** — find 1–2 ATL business content creators in adjacent niches (not competitors); propose a collab monthly
3. **Story replies** — respond to every DM from a "Comment X" trigger within 4 hours; early engagement = algorithm push
