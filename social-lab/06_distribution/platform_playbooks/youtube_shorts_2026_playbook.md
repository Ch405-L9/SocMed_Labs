# youtube_shorts_2026_playbook

## Why YouTube Shorts First for BADGR

YouTube Shorts has the **highest engagement rate of all platforms** for education/tutorial content in 2026 ‚Äî 5.91% platform average, 9.5% for the education niche. Your content (compliance checks, DevTools demos, RAG tutorials) is pure education. This is your highest-upside platform.

Secondary benefit: Shorts feed viewers into YouTube search. A compliance tutorial that performs well as a Short will surface in "HIPAA website" and "ADA web compliance" searches indefinitely ‚Äî compounding return on a single recording session.

## Algorithm Rules (2026)

- **Ranking signals:** Watch-through % (most important) ‚Üí Click-through rate on thumbnail ‚Üí Likes ‚Üí Comments ‚Üí Shares
- Watch-through drives everything ‚Äî if viewers drop at second 5, the video gets buried
- Shorts that get watch-through also get surfaced in the regular YouTube search index over time
- Subscriber conversion: Shorts generate 16.9 subscribers per 10K views ‚Äî lower than long-form but volume is much higher
- Posting consistency matters more than frequency ‚Äî 3x/week beats 7x one week and 0x the next

## Posting Schedule

- Post Shorts 1 day after TikTok/Reels ‚Äî give TikTok the first-mover advantage, then reuse
- Title and description must be rewritten for YouTube (SEO-optimized, not caption-style)
- Mon + Wed + Fri or Tue + Thu + Sat; avoid posting same day as TikTok to prevent duplicate-content signals

## Long-Term Compounding Play
