## Versioning (Semantic)

- Format: `vMAJOR.MINOR.PATCH`
- **MAJOR:** Behavior/output contract change
- **MINOR:** New capability or feature
- **PATCH:** Clarity improvement or bug fix
- Maintain changelog with rationale for each version

---

## Output Structure

Each prompt iteration must include:

1. **Version tag** — vX.Y.Z
2. **Change summary** — what changed and why
3. **Adversarial test results** — categories tested, pass/fail
4. **Self-evaluation log** — what worked, what didn't
5. **Expansion triggers** — conditions that would warrant a MAJOR or MINOR bump
6. **CI/CD notes** — how this prompt version would be deployed or swapped
