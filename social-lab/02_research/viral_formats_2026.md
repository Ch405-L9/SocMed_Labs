## Hook Formula Reference

**Structure:** `[State the problem or result] + [Implication or contrast] + [Promise of resolution]`

| Hook Type | Formula | Example |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| **Metric cold open** | "[Number] [metric]. [Consequence]." | "8-second load time. On mobile. For a law firm." |
| **Mid-sentence start** | Start speaking as if already in progress | "…and this is why your site never ranks." |
| **Bold claim** | "Your [thing] has [problem] right now." | "Your clinic homepage has 3 ADA violations right now." |
| **Myth bust** | "MYTH: '[common belief]'" | "MYTH: 'We can't have forms because of HIPAA.'" |
| **Story start** | "[I/They] did [action]. [Unexpected result]." | "I submitted their contact form. Nothing happened." |
| **Contrast** | "[Thing A] is [X]. [Thing B] isn't." | "Their homepage is compliant. Their contact page isn't." |

**Rule:** The hook must be complete without audio for 30% of viewers (captions are mandatory).

## Platform Algorithm Rules (2026)

### TikTok
- **Ranking signals (in order):** Watch-through rate → Saves → Shares → Comments → Likes
- Trending audio in first 5 seconds: 2–5x distribution boost
- Post at 4–6 PM or 8–10 PM EST for peak delivery
- 3–5 niche hashtags outperform tag-stuffing; avoid >7 tags
- Respond to comments in first 30 minutes to signal active creator

### Instagram Reels
- **Ranking signals:** Saves → Shares → Watch-through → Comments → Likes
- Cross-sharing to Stories adds +15–30% reach on the original post
- Location tags (Atlanta, GA) help local discovery
- Collab posts with complementary accounts extend reach without paid promotion
- Carousel posts outperform Reels for text-heavy compliance content

### YouTube Shorts
- **Ranking signals:** Watch-through % → Click-through rate → Likes → Comments
- Shorts feed users to your long-form library — link related long-form in description
- SEO matters: keyword-rich title + description + 3–5 hashtags
- Highest engagement rate of all platforms for education niche (9.5%)
- Set a custom cover frame manually — the auto-selected frame is almost always wrong

### LinkedIn
- **Ranking signals:** Comments → Shares → Reactions → Dwell time
- Native video (uploaded, not YouTube link) gets 3x more reach than shared links
- Post text-only teaser above the video to hook readers before they press play
- Best B2B posting time: Tue–Thu 9 AM–12 PM EST
- Tag 1–2 relevant people max — more than that looks like spam

## Trending Audio Strategy

**For TikTok and Reels:**
1. Script and record first — don't chase audio during production
2. At posting time, check TikTok Creative Center (Trending Sounds) and Reels Trending Audio
3. Swap to a trending sound from the last 7 days before publishing
4. Compliance/legal content: silence + on-screen text often outperforms music (feels more credible)

**Audio that works for BADGR content:**
- Neutral electronic (no lyrics) — 90% of successful tech tutorials
- Lo-fi beats — works for "day-in-the-life" ops content
- Silence with keyboard sounds — authentic for terminal/DevTools demos

## Posting Cadence Guide

| Stage | Posts/Week | Goal |
|-------|-----------|------|
| 0–20 posts (testing) | 3x/week | Find which series gets saves; don't optimize yet |
| 21–50 posts (learning) | 4x/week | Double down on 1–2 top series; retire what's not saving |
| 51–100 posts (scaling) | 5x/week | Mix proven series; start testing new hooks |
| 100+ posts | 5–7x/week | Introduce new series; repurpose top performers |

**BADGR current stage:** Testing (0–20 posts). Record first, analyze second.

## Content That Ages Well vs. Doesn't

| Ages Well | Expires Quickly |
|-----------|----------------|
| Before/after performance reveals | Posts tied to a specific algorithm update |
| HIPAA/ADA explainers (law changes slowly) | Trending audio-dependent content |
| Tooling demos (Lighthouse, axe, Cursor) | "Here's what's new in [tool]" |
| Myth-bust clips (myths persist) | Platform-specific feature tutorials |
| Checklist content | News-reactive content |

For evergreen content: batch record 3–5 at a time; schedule 2+ days apart.
