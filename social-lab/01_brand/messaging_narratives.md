## Origin Story

1. **Observation:** Local small businesses struggling with outdated websites, manual processes, and confusion about AI tools.
2. **Insight:** Research showed small firms were blocked by cost, lack of expertise, and integration complexity — not lack of motivation.
3. **Decision:** Founded BADGRTechnologies to act as a "technology guide" for small businesses — sitting on the client's side of the table rather than selling them a rigid product.
4. **Proof:** Early projects where simple automations, better infrastructure, or clearer analytics produced measurable gains (time saved, reduced errors, more leads) without enterprise-level budgets.

## Ethical Commitments

- **AI transparency:** Always disclose where AI is used in client solutions (chatbots, decision support, automations) and what data it relies on.
- **Explainability:** Provide plain-English explanations of how AI systems work and their limits.
- **Fairness:** Prefer models and data sources aligned with fairness principles; flag and mitigate known bias risks.
- **Privacy and data minimization:** Collect only the data needed; document retention and access policies clearly.
- **Human review:** Design systems so important decisions are reviewable and overrideable by humans.
