# badgr_phase1_offer

## Offer Summary

**Product:** 14-Day Lead Leak & Compliance Fix
**ICP:** Atlanta law firms (employment, wage & hour, PI) and small medical/dental practices — 5–25 FTEs, owner-led, existing site with visible gaps
**Promise:** In 14 calendar days, BADGR scans, fixes, and documents the revenue-killing issues in your existing website — and delivers a before/after report proving the improvement
**Price Band:** $3,000–$5,000 (flat fee, scope-limited to top 5–10 revenue-impacting fixes)
**Gate Model:** No new offers or clients added until Gate 1 criteria are met (see below)

---

## Delivery Structure: Scan → Fix → Proof

### Phase 1 — Scan (Days 1–3)
- Run CICD Verification Prompt across site
- Lighthouse mobile + desktop scans (LCP, INP, CLS, TTFB)
- ADA/WCAG checks: color contrast, keyboard nav, alt text, landmark structure
- HIPAA web presence flags: PHI in URLs, SSL, privacy policy visibility
- CTA audit: all buttons, forms, and phone links tested manually
- Output: structured findings list, timestamped, page-level

### Phase 2 — Fix (Days 4–10)
- Prioritize by impact × effort; focus first on:
  1. Mobile speed & stability
  2. Working CTAs and forms
  3. Core trust/legal pages
- Fix log: date, page, issue, change description, before/after screenshots
- Human approval required before any production deploy, DNS change, or legal text edit
- Re-run CICD checks after each major fix to confirm flag cleared

### Phase 3 — Proof (Days 11–14)
- Full re-run of Lighthouse scans
- Assemble Lead Leak Compliance Report:
  - Executive summary (business language, no jargon)
  - Before/after metrics table
  - Narrative case notes for 3+ key pages
  - 3 recommended "Next Steps" upsell options
- Draft testimonial request copy
- Verify client folder: scans, fix logs, reports, testimonials, invoice, hours log

## 90-Day Gate Model

### Gate 0 (Start)
- One ICP, one vertical, one offer per 90-day cycle
- No new tools, platforms, or automation until Gate 1
- Daily schedule locked: prospect → email → deliver → build → study → quiz

### Gate 1 (Exit Criteria — must hit ALL before expanding)
- 3–5 paying Atlanta clients completed
- 3+ testimonials collected
- 3+ documented case summaries
- Hours × rate = profitable (not break-even)

### Operating Constitution (7 Rules)
1. Client safety and auditability over speed
2. One ICP and one offer per 90-day sprint
3. No irreversible changes without explicit human approval
4. All client data treated as confidential; no PII to third-party APIs without opt-in
5. No new tools until Gate 1
6. Fix log and compliance report required for every engagement
7. Daily quiz/RAG update is non-negotiable (knowledge compounds)

## Failure Modes & Diagnostics

| Symptom | Likely Cause | Fix |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| No response to 5 daily emails | Wrong ICP or weak subject lines | Rewrite subject + first line; tighten ICP filter |
| Discovery calls not converting | Price anchoring too early | Lead with findings, not price |
| Fix taking >10 days | Scope creep | Re-scope and lock change order in writing |
| Client not providing testimonial | Not asked at the right moment | Ask day 12 during proof walkthrough |
| Lighthouse score unchanged after fix | Wrong fix priority | Re-run CICD; check PageSpeed Insights |
