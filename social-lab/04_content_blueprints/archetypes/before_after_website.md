# before_after_website

## Series: Atlanta Stack Check

**Purpose:** Show before/after website teardowns of anonymous ATL law firm and clinic sites. Establish BADGR as the go-to compliance + performance authority in the market. Drive saves and "Comment X for a free scan" engagement.

**ICP:** Decision-makers at ATL law firms and small medical/dental practices who have an existing website and suspect it has issues — but haven't quantified the problem yet.

**Best Platforms:**
- TikTok (primary — widest cold reach)
- Instagram Reels (primary — saves/shares drive discovery)
- YouTube Shorts (secondary — watch time and search optimization)
- LinkedIn (selective — when compliance angle is strong)

**Ideal Length:** 25–40 seconds

**Visual Style:**
- Faceless screen-share (no camera required)
- Dark browser chrome (Chrome DevTools dark theme preferred)
- Split-screen or sequential before/after — not side-by-side (sequential is cleaner on mobile)
- Captions via Submagic — white text, black strip, bold keywords

**Hook Patterns:**
- State the problem metric cold: "This ATL law firm had an 8-second load time on mobile."
- Lead with the consequence: "53% of mobile visitors leave before this site even loads."
- Counter-intuitive fact: "Their homepage was HIPAA-compliant. Their contact page wasn't."

**Required Assets:**
- Chrome DevTools (always available)
- Lighthouse (Chrome extension or PageSpeed Insights)
- axe DevTools (free Chrome extension for ADA/WCAG)
- Demo site or anonymized client screenshot (never show client name without permission)
- BADGR fix log template (for credibility shot at the end)
- BADGR logo bug PNG (for watermark, bottom-left)

**CTA Variants:**
- "Comment 'SPEED' — I'll run this test on your site."
- "Comment 'CHECK' — free compliance review."
- "Link in bio — book a 10-minute leak scan."
- "Save this — share it with whoever manages your site."
