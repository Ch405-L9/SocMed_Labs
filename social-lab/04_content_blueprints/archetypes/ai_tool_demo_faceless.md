# ai_tool_demo_faceless

## Series: One Prompt, One System / RAG Lab

**Purpose:** Demonstrate that AI automation is accessible, practical, and non-threatening for solo firms. Show Ollama + ChromaDB RAG in action. Drive follows from non-technical business owners curious about AI, and from developers interested in sovereign AI setups.

**ICP:**
- Primary: Solo attorneys and small practice owners who are curious about AI but worried about data privacy
- Secondary: Developers and tech consultants who want to see local RAG implemented in a real workflow

**Best Platforms:**
- YouTube Shorts (primary — educational/tutorial content leads here)
- LinkedIn (primary — B2B owners, decision-makers respond to "here's how this works in your business")
- TikTok (secondary — AI content gets strong engagement when the demo is fast and clear)

**Ideal Length:** 40–60 seconds

**Visual Style:**
- Terminal window showing Ollama running locally (key differentiator: "no cloud, no API bill")
- Python script execution — clean, readable font, dark terminal
- Output shown clearly — readable answer with source citation
- Optional: split-screen showing the prompt vs. the answer

**Hook Patterns:**
- Lead with the business benefit: "What if you could ask your own SOPs a question and get an answer in 2 seconds?"
- Quantify the time save: "This task used to take my paralegal 20 minutes. Now it takes 4 seconds."
- Emphasize privacy: "This runs entirely on your machine. No data leaves your network."

**Required Assets:**
- Ollama installed and running locally (`ollama run mistral` or similar)
- ChromaDB setup with `/social-lab/` docs ingested
- `ask_social_lab.py` query script
- Simple terminal with readable output
- Optional: BADGR Phase 1 ops manual as the knowledge source (demonstrates real-world use)

**CTA Variants:**
- "Comment 'RAG' — I'll share the setup steps."
- "Follow — more sovereign AI setups every week."
- "Comment 'PROMPT' — I'll send you the template."
- "Save this — the full walkthrough is in the bio."
