## Ad 1 — "Cursor-Style Live Fix" (Sign-Up CTA)

**Objective:** Drive click-through to the Lead Leak landing page from cold audiences
**Format:** Vertical video (9:16) + 1:1 static image variants
**Platforms:** TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
**Length:** 25–30s

### Layout
- Dark background (#050505)
- Center: screen recording of browser DevTools + Lighthouse report (before/after transition)
- Bottom-left: BADGR logo bug (small, non-intrusive)
- Bottom-right: "Book Free Scan" button overlay
- Captions: auto-generated via Submagic, white text, black background strip

### Copy Block
| Element | Text |
|---------|------|
| Top headline | "Your Atlanta website is quietly leaking cases." |
| Sub-line | "14-Day Lead Leak & Compliance Fix. Before/after report. HIPAA-aware. Flat fee." |
| CTA button | "Book your free 10-minute leak video." |
| Fine print | "Not legal advice. All templates reviewed by your counsel." |

### Script (Scene-by-Scene)
- **(0–3s) Hook:** Mid-sentence cold open over code editor: "…and this is why your site never makes it past page one." On-screen text: "ATL law firms — watch this."
- **(3–12s) Problem:** Screen shows Lighthouse mobile scan — Performance 31, LCP 6.8s, multiple red flags. VO: "This is what your intake clients see on mobile. A site that takes 7 seconds to load loses 53% of visitors before it even renders."
- **(12–22s) Fix reveal:** Cut to DevTools — image compression applied, render-blocking script deferred. Lighthouse re-run — score jumps to 87. VO: "Two changes. 14 days. Same site."
- **(22–27s) Offer:** Cut to landing page mockup. VO: "BADGR fixes the top 5–10 issues killing your intake calls. Flat fee. Before/after documented."
- **(27–30s) CTA:** On-screen: "Link in bio → Book a free 10-minute leak video." VO: "Book the scan. See what you're losing."

### Platform Placements
| Platform | Aspect Ratio | Max Length | Notes |
|----------|-------------|-----------|-------|
| TikTok | 9:16 | 60s | Use trending audio overlay |
| Instagram Reels | 9:16 | 60s | Add location tag: Atlanta, GA |
| YouTube Shorts | 9:16 | 60s | Add chapter markers if extending |
| LinkedIn | 16:9 or 1:1 | 30s | Use static image variant for sponsored posts |

## Ad 2 — "Solopreneur Promise" (Learn-More / Retarget CTA)

**Objective:** Retarget warm audiences who watched organic videos; drive landing page visits
**Format:** Static motion graphic or 15s looping video
**Platforms:** Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube pre-roll
**Length:** 15–20s (video) or static

### Layout
- Black background (#050505)
- Top text (large, bold, white): "No co-founder. No IT department. No website drama."
- Middle text (medium, #7FA1D3 accent): "Just you, one system, and BADGR handles the rest."
- Bottom tagline: "14-Day Lead Leak & Compliance Fix — Atlanta law and medical practices."
- CTA button: "See the 14-day plan →" (#02068D background, white text)
- Subtle background: faint outline flowchart/org diagram referencing Phase 1 ops timeline
- BADGR logo: bottom-right corner

### Copy Block
| Element | Text |
|---------|------|
| Headline | "No co-founder. No IT department. No website drama." |
| Sub-quote | "Just you, one prompt, and BADGR handles the rest." |
| Tagline | "14-Day Lead Leak & Compliance Fix for Atlanta law and medical practices." |
| CTA button | "See the 14-day plan" |

### Script (Video Variant — 15s Voiceover over Static Visual)
- **(0–3s):** Headline fades in. VO: "Running your firm solo doesn't mean dealing with website problems alone."
- **(3–10s):** Sub-quote appears. VO: "BADGR scans your site, fixes the revenue leaks, and documents everything — in 14 days. Flat fee. HIPAA-aware."
- **(10–14s):** CTA button pulses. VO: "See the full 14-day plan — link below."
