# hooks_and_ctas

Library of proven hook lines (0–3s) and CTA variants organized by series and platform. Pull from here when scripting — don't write from scratch every time.

## CTA Library by Goal

### Drive Saves
- "Save this — you'll want it before your next client meeting."
- "Save this checklist — it takes 5 minutes to run yourself."
- "Bookmark this — I'll update it when the algorithm changes."

### Drive Shares
- "Share this with your practice manager."
- "Send this to whoever runs your website."
- "Tag a colleague who needs to see this."

### Drive Comments (Engagement + Lead Gen)
- "Comment 'SPEED' — I'll run this test on your site for free."
- "Comment 'CHECK' — free compliance review."
- "Comment 'FORM' — fastest way to test your contact form right now."
- "Comment 'RAG' — I'll share the exact setup steps."
- "Comment 'LEGAL' — I'll send you the prompt template."
- "Comment 'HOST' — I'll tell you what to look for in hosting."
- "Comment 'LOG' — I'll share the fix log template."
- "Comment 'PROMPT' — I'll send you the prompt template."

### Drive Link Clicks (Bio / Landing Page)
- "Link in bio — book a free 10-minute leak scan."
- "Link in bio — see the full case summary."
- "Link in bio — free compliance checklist for ATL medical practices."
- "Full walkthrough in the bio — takes 10 minutes."

### Drive Follows
- "Follow for a new fix every week."
- "Follow — one myth debunked per week."
- "Follow for more sovereign AI setups. No cloud required."
