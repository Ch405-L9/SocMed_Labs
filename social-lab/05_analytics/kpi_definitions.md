# kpi_definitions

## Core Metrics

| KPI | Definition | Why It Matters |
|-----|-----------|----------------|
| **Views** | Total plays (including replays) | Top-of-funnel reach indicator |
| **Unique Viewers** | Distinct accounts that watched | True reach; filters out replays |
| **Watch Time %** | Avg % of video watched | Algorithm weight; drives distribution |
| **3-Second Hold Rate** | % of viewers who watch past 3s | Hook effectiveness proxy |
| **Saves** | Times video saved to collection | Strongest algorithm signal on Reels/TikTok |
| **Shares** | Times video sent or reposted | Virality multiplier; drives discovery |
| **Comments** | Total comments | Engagement depth; boosts algorithm |
| **Link Clicks** | Clicks on profile link or bio CTA | Mid-funnel conversion |
| **Calls Booked** | Discovery calls scheduled traceable to content | Bottom-funnel conversion |
| **New Clients** | Paying clients where content was part of the discovery path | Revenue attribution |

| Platform | #1 KPI | #2 KPI | #3 KPI |
|----------|--------|--------|--------|

| Instagram Reels | Saves | Shares | 3-Sec Hold Rate |
| YouTube Shorts | Watch Time % | Saves | Subscribers gained |

## 90-Day Target Ranges (Conservative — First Sprint)

These are baseline targets, not ceilings. Update after 20 posts once actual data exists.

| KPI | Month 1 | Month 2 | Month 3 |
|-----|---------|---------|---------|
| Views/video | 200–500 | 500–2,000 | 2,000–10,000 |
| Watch Time % | >40% | >50% | >60% |
| 3-Sec Hold Rate | >60% | >70% | >75% |
| Save Rate (saves/views) | >1% | >2% | >3% |
| Share Rate (shares/views) | >0.5% | >1% | >2% |
| Link Clicks/video | 5–15 | 15–40 | 40–100 |
| Calls Booked/month | 1 | 2–3 | 4–6 |
| New Clients/month | 0–1 | 1–2 | 2–3 |

## Experiment Decision Rules

- **Kill a hook:** If 3-Sec Hold Rate <40% on 2+ videos using same hook format → retire it
- **Double down on a series:** If save rate >3% on 3+ consecutive videos → increase posting cadence for that series
- **Update the offer CTA:** If link clicks >2% but calls booked <0.5% → landing page or CTA copy is the bottleneck, not content
- **Expand platforms:** After 20 posts on primary platforms with >2% save rate → test one additional platform

---

## Tracking Cadence

- Add experiment_log row within 48 hours of each post going live
- Weekly: review save/share rates, adjust hooks or series mix
- Monthly: review calls booked and new clients; update 90-day targets if needed
- Quarterly: full audit — retire underperforming series, promote winning archetypes
